The Best Movies According to Furkan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather

"The Godfather" is a film that follows the powerful Italian-American crime family of Don Vito Corleone as his youngest son, Michael, reluctantly joins the Mafia and rises to become the new boss. The story explores themes of loyalty, power, and the complexities of family ties.


World War Z

"World War Z" is a film about a former UN investigator, Gerry Lane, who traverses the globe in a race against time to stop a zombie pandemic that threatens to destroy humanity. The story explores themes of survival, global cooperation, and the resilience of the human spirit.


Fast and Furious 1

"The Fast and the Furious" centers on undercover cop Brian O'Conner, who infiltrates the world of illegal street racing to investigate a series of high-profile hijackings. As he gains the trust of the racing crew led by Dominic Toretto, he struggles with his dual loyalties to the law and his new friends.
